ℹ️ How It Works

  • Daily Automation: The system checks every day for loans with due dates = today
  • Automatic Sending: WhatsApp reminders are sent automatically to clients using PyWhatKit
  • Your Number: Messages are sent from your WhatsApp number (you must be logged into WhatsApp Web)
  • Client Numbers: Uses the phone numbers saved in each client's profile
  • One-Time Setup: Configure once, and the system handles everything automatically

⚠️ Important Requirements

  • You must be logged into WhatsApp Web on your computer
  • Keep your computer running during reminder times
  • The system will open WhatsApp Web automatically to send messages
  • Client phone numbers must be valid WhatsApp numbers
  • Test the feature before relying on it for production use
