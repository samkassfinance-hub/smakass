1. Introduction

Welcome to SamKass ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal and business data is handled securely. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the SamKass Finance Manager web application.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you register, we collect your name, email address, business/firm name, and an encrypted password. If you log in using Google OAuth, we collect your basic profile information (name, email, and profile picture).
Business & Financial Data: Information regarding your clients, loan details, EMIs, and payment records.
Usage Data: We may collect basic diagnostic data and usage metrics to improve application performance.

3. How Your Data is Stored

Local Storage Architecture: To ensure high performance and privacy, SamKass is built with an offline-first architecture. Your core business data (clients, loans, payments) is primarily stored locally on your device/browser via localStorage.
Cloud Sync & Authentication: Your account credentials and subscription status are securely managed by our backend servers. Passwords are cryptographically hashed (bcrypt), and sessions are managed via secure JWT cookies.

4. How We Use Your Information

To authenticate your account and secure your data. To provide the core functionalities of the app, including EMI tracking, receipt generation, and WhatsApp reminder formatting. To process subscription upgrades and payments. To send you essential account updates (e.g., Magic Login links, plan expiry notices).

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or business data to third parties. We only share data with essential service providers necessary to operate the app (e.g., Google for OAuth login, email providers for sending login links).

6. User Responsibilities

Because you act as a financier entering data about your clients, you are responsible for obtaining the necessary consent from your clients to store their personal information (such as phone numbers, addresses, and ID numbers) within the SamKass application.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

You have full control over your business data. You can delete individual records or completely clear your local data using the "Clear All Data" feature in the Settings menu. If you wish to permanently delete your SamKass account and cloud data, please contact our support team.
