Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

At KaasFlow, we strive to provide a seamless loan management experience for our users. This Refund and Cancellation Policy outlines our terms regarding the cancellation of subscriptions and our refund process.

1. Subscription Cancellations

You can cancel your KaasFlow premium subscription at any time. When you cancel, you will continue to have access to the premium features until the end of your current paid billing cycle.

  • No Auto-Renewal: Currently, KaasFlow does not automatically renew subscriptions. You must manually renew your plan before it expires to continue premium access.
  • Data Access: Upon expiration of a premium plan, your account will revert to the Free Plan. Your existing data (clients, loans, payments) will remain accessible, but you will not be able to add new clients beyond the 20-client limit.

2. Refund Policy

Because KaasFlow offers a fully functional Free Plan for you to evaluate the software before making a purchase, our standard refund policy is as follows:

  • Monthly & Quarterly Plans: Payments for Monthly and Quarterly subscription plans are final and non-refundable. We do not offer prorated refunds for partial usage.
  • Yearly Plans: We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for our Yearly subscription plan. If you are not completely satisfied within the first 7 days of upgrading, you may request a full refund.
  • Technical Errors & Duplicate Charges: If you are charged multiple times for the same subscription due to a technical error, we will refund the duplicate charge immediately upon verification.

3. How to Request a Refund

If you are eligible for a refund under our 7-day guarantee for Yearly plans, or if you experienced a billing error, please follow these steps:

  • Send an email to support@kaasflow.com from your registered email address.
  • Include your KaasFlow Account Name, Phone Number, and the Razorpay Payment Receipt or Transaction ID.
  • State the reason for your refund request.

Our team will review your request and respond within 2-3 business days. Approved refunds will be processed back to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.

4. Changes to this Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund & Cancellation Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of KaasFlow after any such changes constitutes your acceptance of the new terms.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Refund Policy, please reach out to us at:

  • Email: support@kaasflow.com
