1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, business name
  • Authentication Data: Password/PIN (encrypted), login credentials
  • Payment Information: Payment transaction details for subscription plans (processed securely via Razorpay)

1.2 Client & Loan Data

  • Client Information: Names, phone numbers, addresses, ID numbers, occupation details
  • Loan Records: Principal amounts, interest rates, EMI schedules, payment history
  • Transaction Data: Payment dates, amounts, collection records, receipts

1.3 Usage Data

  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • IP address and location data
  • App usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Error logs and performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: To provide loan management, EMI tracking, and collection features
  • Account Management: To create and maintain your account, process subscriptions
  • Communication: To send payment reminders, notifications, and service updates
  • Analytics: To improve app performance, user experience, and develop new features
  • Security: To protect against fraud, unauthorized access, and security threats
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

3. Data Storage & Security

3.1 Local Storage

KaasFlow stores your business data (clients, loans, payments) locally in your browser's localStorage. This means:

  • Data remains on your device and is not automatically uploaded to our servers
  • You have full control over your data
  • Clearing browser data will delete your local records (use export feature for backups)

3.2 Cloud Storage (Optional)

If you enable cloud sync (premium feature), your data is encrypted and stored securely on our servers with:

  • AES-256 encryption at rest
  • TLS/SSL encryption in transit
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Secure data centers with physical security measures

3.3 Security Measures

  • Password/PIN encryption using industry-standard hashing algorithms
  • Secure payment processing via Razorpay (PCI DSS compliant)
  • Regular security updates and vulnerability patches
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data only in these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors (Razorpay), hosting providers, analytics services
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize data sharing

5. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access: View and download your data anytime using the export feature
  • Correction: Update or correct your information through the app settings
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data by contacting support
  • Data Portability: Export your data in JSON, CSV, or Excel format
